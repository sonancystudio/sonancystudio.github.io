Mission

About
SONANCY.STUDIO is an innovative arts and production company based in Houston, Texas that specialises in creating photos and music with a lurid, captivating edge. The brand has two areas of focus: images combining occult, gothic, macabre themes with the allure and sensuality of boudoir photography; and producing high-quality original music composed of dominating, dark and avant-garde sounds that translate into a powerful, instrumental, sonic experience.
Founded
SONANCY.STUDIO was founded by creative duo Brian Yegge (musician, photographer, digital content creator) and MegAnne Stryker (model, concept and set designer, writer) in July 2020 during the great lockdown. The pair discovered that their ideas and creative passions overlapped in a unique way while working on solo projects in a shared space, resulting in the company's genesis. SONANCY.STUDIO is the brain child conceived from those two joined forces.
Launch
The brand officially launched in February 2021 with the debut photo collection HeXXmaS, alongside the instrumental art rock EP Arete by Angershade (2019).
