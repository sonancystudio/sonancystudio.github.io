Disasterp1ece

A Tribute to Joey Jordison  ·  YeggeStryker Productions  ·  Oct 2022

About

The DISASTERP1ECE is a tribute to Joey Jordison — the founding Slipknot drummer and one of the most technically ferocious drummers of his generation, who passed on July 26, 2021.

This project was primarily spearheaded by MegAnne Stryker as a tribute to the late Joey Jordison. Over the weekends of September 2021, the duo captured over 400 photos.

By October 2022, Brian revisited the project to ensure at least one final image fulfilled the original goal of the tribute.
