HeXXmaS

Status: Archive

HeXXmaS was a promotional campaign for Sonancy.Studio. Commercial use of this content is strictly prohibited. This display is for archive purposes only.

HEX: (v)

To bewitch; practice witchcraft on. (n) A spell; a charm; a curse.

-MAS:

(suffix) A holiday; sacred day; festival; mass.

Creators Brian Yegge and MegAnne Stryker proudly announce HeXXmaS. The series combines elements of black magick and sensuality to illustrate the glamor associated with invoking dark supernatural forces.

The union between occultism and sexuality has existed for as long as magick has been practiced. HeXXmaS captures the enchantment and allure frequently juxtaposed with ritual magick to leave viewers spellbound.
